How to Create a Really Powerful-Delicious-Nugget

Description :

Here we'll tell you how to make a super-ultra-great-delicious-wonderful (chicken) nugget

Ingredient :

  • Chicken Nugget
  • A Stove and an oil

Steps :

  1. Prepare the chicken nugget
  2. Heat the oil with the stove
  3. Add chicken nugget to the stove with oil, wait like around ~5 minutes
  4. Remove and drain the oil from the nugget
  5. NOw you can consume it!!
